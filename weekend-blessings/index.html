Weekend Blessings

Weekend Blessings helps provide single packs of food to students in local schools so they have nourishment at home when they need it most.

What This Ministry Does

This ministry supports close to 200 students across 6 local schools by helping provide practical food support for weekends and school breaks. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Support for Students

The ministry donates and distributes single packs of food to students in local schools who need extra nourishment at home. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Hands-On Service

Volunteers can help by packing bags, shopping for needed items, delivering to schools, and supporting the ministry in practical ways. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Community Giving

Donations can be dropped off at the church in the Weekend Blessings box under the portico by entrance door H, in the Narthex, or in the CAC. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Items Needed for Regular Weeks

Single servings and individually wrapped portions
Best for consistent weekly packing and distribution

During regular school weeks, Weekend Blessings focuses on foods that are easy to distribute, practical for students, and simple for families to use. The live ministry page notes that these weekly donations should use single servings or individually wrapped portions. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

Items Needed for Break Weeks

Additional support for longer times away from school
Designed to help families through extended weekends and breaks

The ministry also highlights a separate set of needs for break weeks, recognizing that school breaks can create extra food pressure for families. This page should make that distinction clear and easy to act on. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Ways to Financially Support

Support the ministry through direct giving and donation-based help
Local impact through school and community partnerships

The live Weekend Blessings page includes a dedicated section for financial support. This gives people another way to serve when they may not be able to donate food items or volunteer in person. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

Get Involved

There are several ways to support this ministry, from volunteering to giving to dropping off needed items. The live page also invites volunteers to reach out by email. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
