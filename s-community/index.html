Serving the Community

Serving the Community helps us love neighbors well through practical outreach, compassionate support, and local partnerships that meet real needs.

How This Ministry Serves

This page highlights practical ways to step outside the church walls, support local families, and live out care through visible acts of service.

Neighbor-Focused Care

Serving the Community creates simple, meaningful ways to show compassion to people nearby through consistent support and practical kindness.

Hands-On Outreach

From local service projects to food support and volunteer opportunities, this ministry helps the church engage people with action, not just intention.

Visible Compassion

These opportunities help the church serve in ways that are practical, relational, and rooted in showing Christ’s love to the surrounding community.

Practical Support

Serve through food support, outreach projects, and meeting everyday needs
Support people in timely, tangible ways that make care visible

Practical support is one of the clearest ways the church can serve the community. It helps connect compassion to action by meeting real needs with consistency and dignity.

Local Relationships

Serve not only through projects, but through presence, trust, and relationship
Care for neighbors in ways that feel personal, steady, and welcoming

Healthy community ministry is relational. It is not just about doing good things, but about showing up faithfully and helping people feel seen, valued, and supported.

Shared Impact

Join with others to serve more consistently and with greater reach
Create a visible witness of service through coordinated local outreach

Serving together multiplies impact. This page can function as a starting point for members who want to find meaningful ways to serve the city, schools, families, and neighbors around them.
