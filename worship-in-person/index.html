Worship In-Person

We keep worship simple for you, and we look forward to seeing you.

When We Meet

We gather on Sundays at 10:00A & 11A.

Worship Services
10:00 & 11:00 a.m.
Our Sunday service lasts about 60 minutes and includes live music and teaching.

Preschool
9:30A & 10:30A
Children ages six weeks through pre-K can enjoy a safe and caring ministry environment during worship.

Kids & Elementary
9:30A & 10:30A
Kids experience music, Bible teaching, and small group connection designed for their age.

Youth
Middle and high school students remain in worship. Check our event calendar for upcoming youth events.

Allow Us to Make Your Sunday Experience Easy

We can make your visit to Kennesaw Church a great and easy experience.

1. Arrive at least 15 to 20 minutes before the service begins if you'd like to be greeted with a gift.

2. Stop by our welcome areas or ask a greeter for help finding classrooms, worship spaces, or your next step.

3. We would look forward to helping you feel connected.

Still have questions, special needs, or requests you'd like to share with us?

Let Us Know

Where We're Located

Our address is 1801 Ben King Rd, Kennesaw, GA 30144.

Where to Park

As you approach the church sign, follow it to the left parking lot. This larger lot gives you easy access and a short walk to both our traditional sanctuary and modern auditorium.

What to Wear

We are casual around here. You will see jeans, shorts, dresses, and business casual attire. Wear something comfortable and come on in.
