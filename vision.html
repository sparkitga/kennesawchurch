Our Vision

Where we're going and what we're called to do

Our Vision

We envision a church where every person experiences the transforming love of Jesus Christ, grows in faith and character, and becomes equipped to serve God and others with their unique gifts and calling.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and make a difference in the world. We accomplish this through worship, teaching, fellowship, and service in our community and beyond.

Our Values

Faith: We believe in the power of God's Word and the work of the Holy Spirit.

Community: We value authentic relationships and doing life together.

Service: We're called to serve our neighbors and make a difference.

Growth: We're committed to growing in faith, character, and love.
