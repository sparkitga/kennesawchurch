About Kennesaw Church

Our story, our mission, and our community

Who We Are

Kennesaw Church is a community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ and sharing His love with our neighbors. We gather to worship, grow in faith, and serve one another and our community.

Our Mission

We exist to make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and make a difference in the world. Through worship, teaching, fellowship, and service, we seek to be a light in our community and beyond.

Join Us

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, you're welcome here. Join us for worship on Sundays, connect with a small group, or get involved in one of our ministries. We'd love to meet you.
