When We Meet

Woodstock City Church has services at 9:00 a.m. and 11:00 a.m. Our typical Sunday schedule is below.

Adults: Services
9:00 & 11:00 a.m.
Our Sunday service lasts about 60 minutes and includes live music and teaching.

Preschool: Waumba Land
9:00 & 11:00 a.m.
Children ages six weeks through pre-K will experience time to play, sing songs, listen to a Bible story, and eat snacks.

Elementary School: UpStreet
9:00 & 11:00 a.m.
Kids learn through music, a story, and small group time.

Middle School: Transit
9:00 & 11:00 a.m.
Middle school students start with worship and teaching, then break into small groups.

High School: InsideOut
4:30 p.m.
Sunday is the best night of the week for high schoolers, with music, a message, and small group time.

Allow Us to Make Your Sunday Experience Easy

We can make your visit to Kennesaw Church a great and easy experience.

1. Arrive at least 30 minutes before the service begins.

2. Approach a Guest Services banner and let us know you're joining us for the first time.

Don't forget to visit us in the Next Room. We can answer questions, help you get connected, and welcome you well.

Still have questions, special needs, or requests you'd like to share with us?

Let Us Know

Please allow 24–48 hours for a response to questions or special requests.

Where We're Located

How to Get Here

Where to Park

As you get closer to the church, look for parking signs and helpful volunteers to guide you. We recommend arriving 30 minutes before service begins to make sure you get in on time.

What to Wear

We are casual around here. You will see jeans, shorts, dresses, and business casual attire. Wear something comfortable and come on.
