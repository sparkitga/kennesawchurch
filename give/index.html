In Person

You can make your gifts in person using cash or checks. Please make checks payable to Kennesaw Church.

You may participate in the weekly offering during our 10:00 or 11:00am services, or drop off your gift at the church office during the week.

Mobile Giving App

Church members can use the Vanco Mobile app to set up an account and give from their phone.

  1. Download the free Vanco Mobile app.
  2. Find Kennesaw Church by name, location, or invite code.
  3. Donate securely.

App Store and Google Play links will be added once mobile giving setup is complete.
