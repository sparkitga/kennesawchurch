Missions

Missions at Kennesaw Church is about sharing Christ’s love through service, partnership, and practical care. We want to be a church that serves our neighbors well and stays open to where God is calling us next.

What to Expect

Three simple ways Missions helps people serve locally and beyond with purpose and compassion.

Local Impact

We look for meaningful ways to serve our local community through practical help, encouragement, and strong partnerships.

Serving Together

Missions creates opportunities for people of different ages and backgrounds to serve together with one shared purpose.

Faith in Action

Every mission opportunity is a chance to reflect Christ through compassion, humility, generosity, and visible care.

Local Missions

Serving schools, families, ministries, and local community partners
Ongoing opportunities throughout the year

Local Missions helps Kennesaw Church stay rooted in service close to home. Whether through outreach projects, partnerships, or hands-on support, the goal is to meet real needs while building relationships that reflect the love of Christ.

Mission Trips

Serving through trips, partnerships, and special outreach opportunities
Seasonal opportunities as trips and partnerships are available

Mission Trips give people a chance to step out, serve faithfully, and support the work God is doing in other places. These experiences are designed to strengthen faith, grow compassion, and help the church stay outward focused in how it lives and serves.

Quick paths to ministries, events, and serving opportunities.
