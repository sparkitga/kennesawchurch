Preschool Ministry

Growing in faith and friendship from the very beginning

Welcome to Our Preschool

Our preschool ministry provides a safe, nurturing environment where young children can learn about God’s love through play, stories, and simple worship. We welcome families with little ones and are here to support you as you raise your children in the faith.

What We Offer

Age-appropriate classes, caring volunteers, and a curriculum that introduces Bible stories and values in ways that stick. Children are grouped by age so activities and lessons fit their stage of development.

Join Us

Preschool programming is available during our Sunday services. For times, room locations, or to get connected, reach out through our Contact page. We’d love to meet you and your family.
