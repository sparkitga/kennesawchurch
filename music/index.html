Music

The Music ministry helps lead our church in worship with excellence, humility, and joy. Through voices, instruments, and faithful service, we seek to help people encounter God and participate fully in worship.

What to Expect

Three simple things you can expect as you connect with Music ministry at Kennesaw Church.

Worship With Purpose

Music ministry is centered on leading people toward Christ through thoughtful, meaningful worship that supports the life of the church.

Preparation & Excellence

Rehearsal, teamwork, and steady preparation help create a worship environment that is both welcoming and well led each week.

A Team-Focused Ministry

This ministry values humility, consistency, and collaboration so every part of the team can serve the church well together.

Vocal Worship

Worship leaders and vocal team support
Weekly worship opportunities through rehearsal and Sunday service

Vocal Worship helps lead the congregation with clarity, warmth, and confidence. This part of the ministry supports worship services by guiding people in song and helping create an atmosphere that is focused on God rather than performance.

Instrumental Team

Band members, accompanists, and worship support
Rehearsal-based service with teamwork and preparation

The Instrumental Team serves by strengthening the worship experience with skill, sensitivity, and consistency. Whether supporting congregational singing or adding depth to special moments, this team helps worship feel unified, strong, and inviting.
