Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM for worship services that include music, prayer, teaching from God's Word, and fellowship. Our worship is designed to help you connect with God and grow in your faith.

What to Expect

Our worship services typically last about 75 minutes and include a mix of contemporary and traditional music, a message from Scripture, and time for prayer and reflection. We welcome everyone, whether you're new to church or have been following Jesus for years.

Join Us

We'd love to have you join us for worship. If you have questions or want to know more, please contact us. You can also watch online if you can't join us in person.
